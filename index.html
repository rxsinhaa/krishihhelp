Our Mission

Our mission is to dismantle the exploitative agricultural supply chain that strips value from the farmer. At Krishi Help, we address a broken system where "distress sales" are the norm, causing smallholders to lose 70% of their product's value to opaque middlemen while $15.2 billion in perishable produce spoils annually. We are an asset-light platform integrator dedicated to returning power and profit to the producer. We provide true market access by transparently replicating the middleman's core services—logistics, aggregation, and finance. Simultaneously, we solve the critical storage gap by partnering with hardware innovators to create "Krishi Help Hubs." This provides farmers with affordable, pay-as-you-go access to modern, solar-powered cold storage, transforming a prohibitive capital cost into a simple operating expense. Krishi Help exists to break the cycle of exploitation, eliminate waste, and ensure that the farmer, not the aggregator, captures the true value of their harvest.

From smart irrigation to crop health monitoring, our tools are designed to be simple, effective, and accessible to everyone.

The KrishiHelp platform

Krishi Help is a mobile-first platform designed for the Indian farmer, built to run on the nation's widespread 4G/5G rural network. Our solution directly attacks the two root causes of farmer exploitation: Market Linkage: The app digitizes and commoditizes all core middleman services—logistics, aggregation, and finance—making them transparent and accessible to the farmer. Storage Access: We solve the $15.2 billion spoilage problem with our asset-light "Krishi Help Hubs." Farmers use the app to instantly book pay-as-you-go space in a local, partnered solar-powered cold storage unit, eliminating the "distress sale" and the high cost of ownership. With technology access now universal, our intuitive platform focuses on driving adoption, placing the power to control the harvest-to-market journey directly into the farmer's hands.

"Using Krishi-Help transformed our small farm. Our crop yield increased by 30% in just one season. It's like having an agronomist in your pocket."

Market and KrishiHelp

Based on market analysis, the primary market for "Krishi Help" is India's large population of smallholder farmers, who often face exploitation by middlemen and are forced into low-price "distress sales". This is compounded by an estimated $15.2 billion in annual post-harvest losses for perishable goods like fruits and vegetables.   Your app can serve two main markets: Producers: Smallholder farmers, aggregated through Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) to streamline logistics and selling.   Buyers: A B2B market of retailers, hotels, and food processors , as well as a direct-to-consumer (B2C) market for local customers.   Market research shows this is highly feasible due to rapidly expanding rural smartphone penetration and 4G/5G availability. The key challenge identified is low digital literacy , meaning the app must be simple, icon-based, and ideally feature voice commands in local languages.
